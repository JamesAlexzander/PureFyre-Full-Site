Pure Fyre Genetics is a licensed cultivation facility in southern California specializing in growing exotic strains to further enrich the cannabis culture.

With our state of the art facilities we are able to not only grow some of the best exotic strains of cannabis but we are able the ensure that each batch is grown with the quality and care that a connoisseur grower demands but at a commercial scale.
